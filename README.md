# Engineering Design & Simulation Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio repository. This space showcases my academic engineering projects and ongoing research in design, manufacturing, and motorsport performance engineering.

---

## 📂 Project Index

### 💨 [01: Hair Dryer Design for Manufacture (DFM) & Tooling Optimization](./01_Hair_Dryer_Design)
An end-to-end engineering project focusing on the design, mold flow simulation, and CNC manufacturing preparation of a consumer product housing using PTC Creo and ANSYS.
* **Core Focus:** Uniform $3\text{ mm}$ wall thickness compliance, $1^\circ$ draft angle optimization, and custom runner/gate iteration to eliminate air traps.
* **Manufacturing:** Advanced 3-axis CNC toolpath generation ($2000\text{ RPM}$) and G-code validation for a functional two-part injection mold cavity.

### 🏁 [02: F1 Brake Duct Flow & Thermal Optimization](./02_F1_Brake_Duct)
*(In Active Development)*  
An aerodynamic and thermal management study optimizing Formula 1 brake duct intake and internal geometry.
* **Core Focus:** Balancing aerodynamic drag minimization with optimal mass flow rate requirements for caliper and rotor thermal cooling.
